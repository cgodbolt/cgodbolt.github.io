Activities We Offer

Our carefully planned program allows children to choose from a wide range of play and learning experiences and in doing so, help them recognize, understand and express their own emotions as well as to sympathize and respect emotions of others.

The variety of experiences include opportunities for creative self-expression through media such as dance, musical rhythm, and art; development of manipulative skills and motor coordination through puzzles, blocks, bikes, and climbing equipment; the increase of knowledge through excursions, cooking, and science activities; the development of concepts through exposure to materials which stimulate basic awareness; the growth of language skills through stories, song, and dramatic play.

Classrooms:

Each of the classrooms at Kids Academy is set up with learning centers. These centers include Reading, Literacy and Language, Manipulatives, Discovery, Imaginative Play, Blocks, Creative Expression, and Music. The possibilities for learning are endless in these centers, as they are filled with age-appropriate toys, games, and activities to facilitate learning. Our daily routine includes Large Group Time, Small Group Time, Music and Movement, Snack, Outdoor Play, Lunch, Nap, Story Time, and much more.

Ages:

  • Infant: 6 weeks to 12 months and walking.
  • Toddler 1 and Toddler 2: Programs are separated by small groups/classrooms by ages 12-18 months and 18-24 months. Toilet training begins at 24 months.
  • 2’s and Early Pre-K: Programs are separated by small groups/classrooms by ages 24-30 months and 30-36 months.
  • Pre-K3: Children must be 3 years of age to enter. Child must be fully potty trained.
  • Pre-K4: This program is designed for children 3-5 years. A complete Preschool Curriculum year round.
  • VPK: Kids Academy is an approved VPK provider. Children must be 4 years of age by Sept. 1st of each VPK year to attend. VPK is FREE. Extended care programs are available and vary by location.
  • Pre-K Reading Camp: This program is offered during the summer in 4 or 8 week sessions. Utilizing components of the VPK standards, your child will continue learning key concepts and skills that will make them successful readers. The focus in this camp is “Emergent Literacy”. This program is available at select locations.
  • School Age: Program includes transportation, snack, homework time, games, outdoor play, field trips, and more.
  • Adventure Camp: A complete program for children ages 5-12. Full days off, Winter, Spring, and Summer Camp!
